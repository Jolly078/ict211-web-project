About Grill n’ Chill

Grill n’ Chill is a fictional restaurant project designed by Group 28 as part of our ICT 211 Web Design assignment. It's a showcase of our skills in HTML, CSS, and a little JavaScript.

Our Mission

To combine creativity, design, and clean code to build a visually appealing and functional restaurant website.

Project Objective

This website demonstrates our ability to structure a multi-page site, apply good styling practices, and collaborate as a team using modern tools like GitHub.
